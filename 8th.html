Lunch project - Student Workbook

When I found out that the school where I previously worked at was going to launch a Project Based Learning curriculum, I was one of the few who volunteered to take the course. I was really excited to bring this to my students!

After learning about steps that I needed to take, I carefully designed the project. First, I needed to know what would motivate my students - and it was Lunch! I then incorporated it in the design and I never saw them so engaged in their projects! My students even presented their ideas to the principal and I was so proud of them.

This is my sincere hope that this would also support your students to be excited and engaged in this project.
