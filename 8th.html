Lunch project - Student Workbook

Ever since I have read the book, “Reading and Writing the World with Mathematics,” along with taking several classes about race, history, and Latina/o Studies at a graduate school, one of my goals has been to support critical consciousness of my students.

One day, I watched a video about race and racism and was struck by this statement: There is a disparity of trees between different neighborhoods. This led me to conduct research and create a project for students to develop their critical consciousness and participate in civic engagement. I hope this contributes you and your students to take a step toward creating equity and social justice in your classroom.
